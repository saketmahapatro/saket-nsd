The State of Liberty : Jan 2026

As of January 10, 2026, Captain America (Sam Wilson) has established the Global Response Unit. Following the 2025 discovery of Adamantium in the remains of the Celestial Tiamut, the world stands on the brink of a new global conflict.

Reports from Captain America: Brave New World indicate that the mantle of Captain America is no longer purely military, but diplomatic. Sam Wilson is currently mediating between Wakanda and the United States to prevent the weaponization of the new metal.
