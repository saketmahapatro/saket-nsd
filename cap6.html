Shield Laboratory Specifications



Chemical Composition

Proto-Adamantium (Comics): An accidental alloy of Vibranium, Steel, and an unknown catalyst created by Myron MacLain. It has never been duplicated.

Pure Vibranium (MCU): Howard Stark forged the shield using the entirety of the U.S. government's Vibranium supply in 1943. It absorbs 100% of all kinetic energy.

Aerodynamic Data

Ricochet Geometry: The shield's concave shape acts as an airfoil. It creates its own lift, allowing it to maintain speed even after bouncing off multiple surfaces.

Balance: The center of gravity is exactly in the middle of the 30-inch diameter, allowing it to be thrown as a spinning blade or a blunt disc.
