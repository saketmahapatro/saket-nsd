Total Mission Chronology

1941: The Genesis

Real World: Joe Simon and Jack Kirby create Steve Rogers for Timely Comics.
Fiction: Steve Rogers receives the Super Soldier Serum in a secret Brooklyn bunker. Dr. Abraham Erskine is assassinated, leaving Steve as the sole successful subject.

1942 - 1944: The WWII Theater

Rogers leads the Howling Commandos across Europe. Key battles include the rescue of Bucky Barnes and the destruction of Baron Zemo's sonic weaponry.

1945: The Frozen Sleep

Steve Rogers stops Red Skull's Valkyrie bomber from destroying New York. The plane crashes in the Arctic. Rogers enters cryogenic stasis.

1964: Silver Age Rebirth

In Earth-616 continuity, Rogers is found by the Avengers and becomes their field leader.

2011: MCU Awakening

S.H.I.E.L.D. recovers Rogers. He awakens in modern-day Times Square and leads the Avengers.

2014 - 2018: Civil War & Exile

The Sokovia Accords divide the Avengers. Rogers operates underground as Nomad until Thanos arrives.

2019 - 2023: The Endgame

Rogers returns the Infinity Stones and retires to live with Peggy Carter.

2024 - 2026: Present Day

Sam Wilson leads a new roster of Avengers as multiversal threats emerge.
